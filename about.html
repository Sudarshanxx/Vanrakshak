About Vanarakshak

Our Mission

To leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence and satellite imagery to monitor, detect, and prevent deforestation and wildlife poaching across India’s forest landscapes, ensuring sustainable conservation for future generations.

Our Vision

A future where technology and nature coexist, where every tree and every wild animal is protected by a vigilant, intelligent guardian from above—Vanarakshak.
